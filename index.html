About the Firm

Practice built on rigor. Relationships built on trust.

SR Tax Consulting was founded in Bengaluru in 2009 with a singular focus: provide counsel that is honest, thorough, and built to last. We are not generalists who happen to know tax law. We are specialists who understand that behind every balance sheet is a decision someone is trying to make.

Our team of qualified Chartered Accountants works across audit, taxation, corporate compliance, and advisory — with the kind of depth that only comes from years of focused practice.

We do not chase volume. We maintain a deliberate client roster because we believe the quality of our attention is the most valuable thing we offer.

What We Do

Comprehensive. Clear. Compliant.

We offer the full spectrum of chartered accountancy services — structured to adapt to your scale, sector, and stage of growth.

01 / taxation

Direct & Indirect Taxation

ITR filing, advance tax planning, GST registration and returns, TDS compliance, and representation before tax authorities. We handle complexity so your business does not have to absorb it.

02 / audit

Audit & Assurance

Statutory audits, internal audits, concurrent audits, and stock audits conducted with methodical thoroughness. Our reports are written to be read and acted on.

03 / compliance

Corporate Compliance & ROC Filing

Company incorporations, annual filings, board resolutions, MCA compliance, and secretarial support under the Companies Act, 2013. Deadlines are met without reminders.

04 / advisory

Financial Advisory & CFO Services

Fractional CFO support for growing businesses — including financial modelling, MIS reporting, cash flow planning, and investor-ready financials.

05 / setup

Business Setup & Structuring

Entity selection, LLP formation, MSME registration, startup recognition, and cross-border structuring advice. We help you start right.

06 / bookkeeping

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Monthly, quarterly, and annual bookkeeping managed remotely or on-site. Tally, QuickBooks, Zoho Books — we work in your environment.
