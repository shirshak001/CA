What We Do

Comprehensive. Clear. Compliant.

We offer the full spectrum of chartered accountancy services — structured to adapt to your scale, sector, and stage of growth.

01 / taxation

Income Tax & GST Filing

ITR filing for salaried professionals and businesses. GST registration, monthly returns, and TDS compliance. We file accurately, on time, and represent you if the department sends a notice.

02 / audit

Statutory & Internal Audits

Complete audit services: statutory audits, tax audits, internal control reviews, and stock verification. Our audit reports are written to be clear, actionable, and compliant with every ICAI standard.

03 / compliance

Company Registration & ROC Filing

Private limited company incorporation, LLP registration, annual ROC filings, board resolutions, and MCA compliance. We handle the paperwork from day one so your company stays in good standing.

04 / advisory

CFO Services & Financial Planning

Part-time CFO support for growing businesses. We prepare MIS reports, manage cash flow, build investor-ready financial models, and keep your finance function running without a full-time hire.

05 / setup

Business Registration & Structuring

Starting a business in Kolkata? We handle MSME registration, startup recognition, entity selection (Pvt Ltd vs LLP vs OPC), and all government filings to get you operational in under 10 working days.

06 / bookkeeping

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Monthly and quarterly bookkeeping managed remotely or on-site. We work with Tally, QuickBooks, and Zoho Books — so your books are always current, reconciled, and ready for any audit.
